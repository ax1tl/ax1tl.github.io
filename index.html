Really important stuff is about to happen to the site! And by that, I mean that I'm going to be doing a complete overhaul of everything.

A lot of this site is currently just me messing around instead of serving an actual function. For this reason, I'm going to be scrapping the old look, style, and code. In its place, I want to create an actual portfolio for my music and projects. I'm going to have music front and center, with everything else on the side.

__ "What does this mean for the small tools and tidbits?"

Tools like The Unicode Library and Notepad will still be available! I don't plan on getting rid of anything from the old site.


__ "What will the new site look like?"

Like I said, I want to create an actual portfolio for my music. The homepage will display a small blurb about me before showing either 6 or 9 snippets and what they showcase best. From there, I'll provide a few testimonials from friends and clients and a way to contact me.

I'll have a seperate page for my music projects, including quick links to those projects on other sites, and pages for each project that goes a bit more in depth.

Hopefully this won't take too long for me to do as I desperately need a site that does what I want it to. Sorry if this affects anything!

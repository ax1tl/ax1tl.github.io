This is my submission for the annual chips compo event: ASS2022
Taken from my chips compo submission: https://chipscompo.com/entry/2572

Dear avarti / artisiole,

You asked for "a mix between lowercase and noise"

What the hell.

Ignoring the polar opposite genres, I'm happy to say I survived your request!
I asked for clarification on the request and you returned with "the request is
asking for a blend between the genres lowercase and noise, so i'd maybe
recommend starting by making one of the genres individually, then fusing
elements of the other into it" So after about 8 days of trying to make noise
music (with 0 knowledge about it), I gave up and started to do actual research
on the genre. I genuinely started to appreciate what the genre had to offer.
(I did this for a bout an hour btw) With a fresh mind and new ideas, I got
back to work. Taking a more subtle approach, I decided to go after the
uneasiness that both genres seem to have, with more atmospheric sound then
actual white noise.


I spent around 10-ish days for this and had 5 previous failed attempts. It
sounds nice considering the two genres but I will forever be unsatisfied :)


I present to you, "grit."
